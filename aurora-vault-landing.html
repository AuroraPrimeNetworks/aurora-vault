FAQ

Common Questions

Is my group's money safe on Aurora Vault? +
Aurora Vault does not hold your money. All payments go directly between members and your group's MTN MoMo account. We only track and display the transactions. Your money is always in your group's wallet.
What happens after the 30-day free trial? +
After 30 days you can choose a paid plan that suits your group size. If you don't upgrade, your account is paused — your data is kept safely for 90 days so you can resume anytime.
Do members need smartphones to use Aurora Vault? +
Members need a smartphone with internet access to log in and view their savings. However, MoMo deposits can be triggered by the admin on behalf of members using USSD — so even basic phone users can participate.
Can I migrate my existing Excel data to Aurora Vault? +
Yes! When you register, our team helps you import your existing savings records from Excel so you don't lose any historical data. This is included in all plans at no extra cost.
What is the 1% transaction fee? +
For every MoMo deposit a member makes through Aurora Vault, 1% goes to Aurora Prime Networks to keep the platform running and improving. For example, a UGX 10,000 deposit means UGX 100 goes to Aurora Vault — the member receives UGX 9,900 in their group savings.
Can I use Aurora Vault for a SACCO or chama? +
Absolutely! Aurora Vault works for any type of savings group — informal groups, SACCOs, chamas, investment clubs and more. The platform is fully customizable to fit your group's rules.
