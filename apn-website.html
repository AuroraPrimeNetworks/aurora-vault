About APN

We Build Digital Infrastructure
for Real Impact

Aurora Prime Networks (APN) is a Ugandan technology infrastructure company founded in 2026. We design and build digital products and platforms that empower communities, organizations and businesses to operate more efficiently — across any sector.

We started with a simple problem: MAPSAA, a savings group of 15 people, was managing UGX millions on Excel spreadsheets. We built them a proper digital platform — and realized thousands of communities had the same problem across different sectors.

That insight became Aurora Vault — our first product. And that first product became the foundation of a broader vision: Aurora Prime Networks as a digital infrastructure company that can serve any community or sector that needs better tools.

We are not just building apps. We are building digital infrastructure — the reliable, scalable layer that connects people, organizations and communities to the tools they need to grow.

Mission & Vision

What We Stand For

🎯
Our Mission
To democratize access to professional digital tools for every community, organization and business in Africa — regardless of sector, size, location or technical knowledge.
  • Make community operations transparent and accountable
  • Replace manual processes with reliable digital systems
  • Connect organizations to the infrastructure they need
  • Empower leaders with real data and digital tools
🔭
Our Vision
To become Africa's leading digital infrastructure company — the backbone that powers how millions of Africans work, organize, save and grow across every sector of life.
  • 10,000+ organizations powered by APN products by 2028
  • Expand across East Africa and beyond
  • Launch products across finance, education and health sectors
  • Build Africa's most trusted digital infrastructure brand
